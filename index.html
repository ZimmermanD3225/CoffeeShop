Franchise Strategy

7 Brew uses Area Development Agreements (ADAs) - selling exclusive territory rights for entire states or regions to large franchise groups who commit to building dozens or hundreds of stands.

Blackstone Growth invested in Feb 2024 (when chain had ~190 locations). Previously backed by Jimmy John Liautaud (Jimmy John's founder) who exited. ~2,500 future stands under development agreements. No longer accepting new franchise applications.

The Modular Build

All stands are ~510 sq ft prefab modular units built by Creative Modular Construction (CMC) in Springfield, MO. Double drive-thru lanes, no indoor seating.

Built in-factory in 8-10 weeks (vs. 18-20 weeks traditional), trucked to site, and crane-dropped onto prepared foundations.

CMC produces ~2 buildings/week and is building a $30M expansion to keep up with demand.
